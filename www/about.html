The Surf Station Weather Station is located in St. Augustine Beach, FL 32080 at GPS: 29° 50.811' N; 81° 15.968' W - 40 FT.
The main station is a Davis Wireless Vantage Pro2™ (Part #: 06152). We connect the main station via a Davis WeatherLinkIP™ for Vantage Stations (Part #: 06555) to a computer running the wview weather data management software to store data history and generate charts.
